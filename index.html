Quick Start
  1. View the demo dashboard to explore all features
  2. Click Register to create your own account
  3. Login with your email and password
  4. Click the + button to add your first transaction
  5. View your updated Dashboard — cards and charts update in real time
  6. Go to Budgets to set spending limits per category
  7. Create Goals to track savings targets
  8. All data is saved automatically in your browser
